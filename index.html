How to Play

On Your Turn

You get 3 Action Points per turn.

  • 1 AP: Draw a card from the deck
  • 1 AP: Play a Hero, Item, or Magic card from your hand (Hero: may roll immediately)
  • 1 AP: Roll to use a Hero card's effect in your Party (once per Hero per turn)
  • 2 AP: Attack a Monster card
  • 3 AP: Discard your hand and draw 5 new cards

Winning

First to collect 3 Monsters OR have 6 different Hero class cards in your Party wins!

Attacking Monsters

Check the Monster card for required Hero types. Roll the dice – if you meet/beat the win threshold, SLAY it. If you meet/beat the lose threshold, suffer the penalty.

Challenges

Any player may play a CHALLENGE card when you play a Hero, Item, or Magic card. Both players roll – higher roll wins. Loser's card is cancelled / challenger discards.

Keywords

  • DRAW – Take from the top of the deck
  • DISCARD – Move to discard pile
  • STEAL – Take a Hero from another party to yours
  • DESTROY – Move a Hero to the discard pile
  • SACRIFICE – Move one of YOUR Heroes to the discard pile
  • SLAY – Remove a Monster from the row and add to your collection
